What is BetIQ?

BetIQ is an AI-powered sports bet analyzer that evaluates every leg of your bet slip using current player statistics, matchup data, and historical trends — then gives you an honest probability of winning.

Most bettors make decisions based on gut feel, social media tips, or sportsbook marketing. BetIQ replaces guesswork with data. You get a letter grade (A through F), an expected value rating (+EV or -EV), and a hit probability percentage for every single leg.

Think of it as having a professional analyst review your bet before you lock it in — in under 5 seconds.

// The Process
Four Steps to Smarter Bets
STEP 01
📸
Upload Your Bet Slip
Take a screenshot of your bet slip from any sportsbook — DraftKings, FanDuel, BetMGM, Caesars, or any other. Upload the image and BetIQ reads every player, line, and odd automatically. No typing required.
STEP 02
🧠
AI Reads the Data
Our AI instantly pulls season averages, last 10 game performance, head-to-head matchup history, injury reports, home/away splits, and situational trends for every player on your slip.
STEP 03
📊
Probability Calculated
Each leg receives a precise hit probability percentage based on statistical modeling. The AI then compares your probability against the sportsbook's implied odds to determine if each bet is +EV or -EV.
STEP 04
✅
Get Your Grade
Your full slip receives a letter grade from A to F, an overall probability, an EV summary, and a detailed breakdown of every leg — with specific stats, positive factors, and risks spelled out clearly.
