Frequently Asked Questions
Is BetIQ free to try? ▼
Yes. The Free plan gives you 3 analyses per day with no credit card required. You can test every feature before upgrading.
Can I cancel anytime? ▼
Absolutely. There are no contracts or lock-ins. Cancel your subscription at any time and you'll retain access until the end of your billing period.
How accurate are the probability ratings? ▼
BetIQ's AI uses current season statistics, recent form, matchup history, and situational data to calculate probabilities. No model is perfect, but every analysis is based on real data — not gut feeling or marketing hype.
What sportsbooks does BetIQ support? ▼
BetIQ works with any sportsbook. Just screenshot your bet slip — it reads DraftKings, FanDuel, BetMGM, Caesars, PointsBet, and any other app automatically.
Does upgrading happen instantly? ▼
Yes. Once payment is confirmed your plan upgrades immediately and your daily limit resets to unlimited.
